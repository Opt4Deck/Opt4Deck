# About the Author - Giannis Serafeim
<div align='justify'>
   <p><ins>Giannis Serafeim</ins> is a Mechanical Engineer and a graduate of the National Technical University of Athens (2016). He completed his Ph.D. in 2023 with the dissertation titled <i>'Multi-disciplinary Design Optimization of Wind Turbine Blades Including Passive Load Control Techniques'</i>. His research and professional activities include, among others, floating wind turbine design, aeroelastic analysis, fatigue assessment and Life-Time estimation of wind farms, as well as techno-economic evaluation of complex energy systems.</p>
   <p>He has developed computational tools that integrate numerical methods and optimization algorithms, aimed at solving high-complexity engineering problems. His professional background includes collaborations with iWIND Renewables S.A. and the Energy Competence Center, as well as participation in European research projects such as INNWIND.EU.</p>
   <p>He is the creator and lead developer of <strong>Opt4Deck</strong>, a modular optimization-algorithm platform designed for applications in mechanical design, academic research, and industrial analysis.</p>
